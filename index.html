We are a group of MIT students and alumni that are dedicated to building high performance combat robots of all shapes and sizes.

Our robots range from 1lb antweights to 250lb heavyweights and compete in competitions across the United States. Our most recent endevour, Uppercut, will be competing on BattleBots.
